LIFE

Conway's game of life displayed in the terminal via curses.

Basics

python life to start a blank game for editing, python life <filename> to load a file with a pre-defined game state.

File Format

Game states are in JSON format and contain a list of live cells in that state: 
 {
  "cells": [
    {"y": 2, "x": 2}, 
    {"y": 3, "x": 3}, 
    {"y": 4, "x": 3}, 
    {"y": 4, "x": 2}, 
    {"y": 4, "x": 1}
  ]
}
Cell locations will be shifted on load to be centered relative to the current window size. Some sample patterns are in patterns/.

Controls

Move cursor . . . . Arrow keys, WASD or KJHL

Paint/delete cell . . . . . . SPACE or ENTER

Next generation . . . . . . . . . . . . .  G

Run . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .  R

Change tick speed  . . . . . . . . . . . +/-

Output to file . . . . . . . . . . . . . . F

Display help . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . I
